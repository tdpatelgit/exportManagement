Pick a product once, then use "Add design(s)" to tick as many designs as you need under it — they stay grouped in one product block here, but each still prints as its own numbered line on the packing list. Boxes is compulsory per design; Qty fills in automatically. Picking a product fills in Unit, the per-box figures and the Pallet type options from its packing spec (shared by every design under it). Pallet type starts on "Loose" — no pallets; choosing one of the product's pallet types works out Plts from Boxes (or Boxes from Plts) using that pallet's box capacity, and the dropdown shows how much each pallet type stores. Pcs and Qty follow from Boxes (or a "2 PCS = 0.72 SQM" note in the product description). Every auto-filled value stays editable.
